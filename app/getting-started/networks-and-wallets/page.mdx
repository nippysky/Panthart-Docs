# Networks & Wallets

Panthart runs on **Electroneum EVM**.

## Add Electroneum to MetaMask
1. Open MetaMask → **Networks** → **Add network**.
2. Enter RPC details (chain ID, currency symbol).  
3. Save, then switch to Electroneum.

> **Security:** Always verify RPC/chain IDs from official Electroneum sources.

## Supported wallets
- **MetaMask** (desktop & mobile dapp browsers)
- Any wallet that supports custom EVM networks and standard `eth_*` RPC

## Troubleshooting
- **“Wrong network”** banner: switch to Electroneum in your wallet.
- **Nonce too high/low**: reset account in wallet → try again.

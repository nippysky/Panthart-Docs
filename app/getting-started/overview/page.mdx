# Overview

Panthart lets you **launch**, **list**, and **trade** NFTs on **Electroneum EVM**, supporting both **ERC-721** and **ERC-1155**.  
Creators get clean minting flows; collectors get fast, low-friction purchases and auctions. Everything settles **on-chain**.

<Callout type="info">
Non-custodial by design: your wallet stays in your control. Settlement sends NFT → buyer, fees/royalties → recipients, and proceeds → seller <em>atomically</em>.
</Callout>

## What you can create

<Tabs items={['721 Drop', 'Single 721', 'Single 1155']}>

  <Tabs.Tab>
  ### Collection (ERC-721 Drop)
  Launch a collection with **allowlist/presale** and **public mint**, or self-mint items now and open the drop later.  
  Common uses: full collections, phased reveals, community allowlists.

  **Highlights**
  - Supply cap, presale window, public mint, optional reveal
  - Creator controls on metadata/reveal and mint schedule
  - Plays nicely with marketplaces and wallets

  **Go deeper →** [/creators/create-nft/erc721-drop](/creators/create-nft/erc721-drop)
  </Tabs.Tab>

  <Tabs.Tab>
  ### Single NFT (ERC-721)
  Mint a **true 1/1** with unique metadata—ideal for single artworks or high-value collectibles.

  **Highlights**
  - One-of-one provenance
  - Immediate fixed-price listing or timed auction
  - Royalty policy honored where configured

  **Go deeper →** [/creators/create-nft/erc721-single](/creators/create-nft/erc721-single)
  </Tabs.Tab>

  <Tabs.Tab>
  ### Single (ERC-1155)
  Create one token with **many editions** (shared metadata).  
  Perfect for passes, merch, game items, and open editions.

  **Highlights**
  - Total supply you control
  - Flexible distribution (creator-minted or public)
  - Efficient transfers and listings

  **Go deeper →** [/creators/create-nft/erc1155-single](/creators/create-nft/erc1155-single)
  </Tabs.Tab>

</Tabs>

## Fees (at a glance)

- **Marketplace fee:** **2.5%** per trade — **1.5% → Reward Distributor** (for Non-Fungible Comrades holders), **1% → Panthart**  
- **Creation fee:** small, shown in the UI **before** mint; covers **IPFS pinning/bandwidth** and convenience services  
- **Gas:** paid by the caller to the network on every transaction (ETN, or ERC-20 approvals where relevant)

<Callout type="warning">
Listing currency = settlement currency. If you list in an ERC-20, fees/royalties/proceeds are paid in that token. First-time ERC-20 use requires a standard <code>approve</code> to the marketplace contract.
</Callout>

**Learn more**  
- Creators → [/creators/royalties-fees](/creators/royalties-fees)  
- Governance → [/governance-and-policies/fees-schedule](/governance-and-policies/fees-schedule)

## Admin & security (how the protocol stays safe)

- **Multisig Admin (2-of-N):** protocol-level changes (fee wallets/params, pausable modules, distributor settings) require **two signatures**.  
- **Reward Distributor:** receives **1.5%** of each trade and makes pro-rata rewards claimable by **Non-Fungible Comrades** holders.  
- **Contract transparency:** all addresses live at **References →** [/references/contract-addresses](/references/contract-addresses); ABIs at **References →** [/references/abis](/references/abis).

<Callout>
Always confirm the spender/contract in wallet prompts matches the addresses in References. If something looks unfamiliar, cancel.
</Callout>

## From zero to first trade

<Steps>
  <ol>
    <li><strong>Connect wallet</strong> — switch to Electroneum EVM if prompted.</li>
    <li><strong>Create</strong> — pick <em>721 Drop</em>, <em>Single 721</em>, or <em>Single 1155</em>; upload media/metadata.</li>
    <li><strong>List</strong> — fixed price or timed auction; confirm approvals if it’s your first time.</li>
    <li><strong>Sell & settle</strong> — sale finalizes on-chain; fees/royalties distribute; proceeds hit your wallet.</li>
  </ol>
</Steps>

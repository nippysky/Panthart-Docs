## Fee impact per trade

<FeeCalculator />

> Panthart charges a **2.5%** marketplace fee per trade: **1.5%** flows to the **Reward Distributor** for Non-Fungible Comrades holders and **1%** to the Panthart fee wallet. Use the calculator above to see examples.

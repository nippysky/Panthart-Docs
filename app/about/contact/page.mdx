- **Docs fixes** → PRs to [panthart-docs](https://github.com/nippysky/panthart-docs)

> Add context (links to collection, tx hash, screenshots, console errors) so we can help faster.

---

## Security & responsible disclosure

If you discover a vulnerability, **do not** post it publicly.

1. Message an **admin** in the Telegram group to request a **private channel** for disclosure.  
2. Share clear reproduction steps, expected vs. actual behavior, and potential impact.  
3. We’ll acknowledge promptly and coordinate a safe fix/credit.

> We never ask for your seed phrase or private keys. If anyone does, it’s not us.

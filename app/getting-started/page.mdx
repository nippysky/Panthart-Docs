# Getting Started

Panthart is an NFT marketplace for **ERC-721** and **ERC-1155** on **Electroneum EVM**.  
This guide takes you from zero to your first **mint**, **listing**, and **trade**—safely and quickly.

<Callout type="warning">
Always verify you are on <strong>Electroneum EVM</strong> and interacting with the official contracts listed in  
<strong>References → <a href="/references/contract-addresses">Contract Addresses</a></strong>. If your wallet shows an unknown spender or address, <strong>cancel</strong>.
</Callout>

---

## Who this is for

- **Creators** — launch a 721 **Drop**, mint a **Single 721**, or create a **Single 1155** edition.  
- **Collectors** — buy fixed-price items or bid in timed auctions.

---

## What you’ll need

- A browser wallet (e.g., **MetaMask**, WalletConnect-compatible) connected to **Electroneum EVM**.  
- A small amount of ETN (or a supported **ERC-20** for listings/auctions) for **gas**.  
- **Creators:** your media files and basic metadata. The **creation fee** covers **IPFS** pinning and convenience services.

<Callout type="info">
Multi-currency is supported. Listing currency = settlement currency.  
First-time ERC-20 usage requires a one-time <code>approve</code> for the marketplace contract.
</Callout>

---

## 2-minute setup

<Steps>
  <ol>
    <li><strong>Connect wallet</strong> — open Panthart and click <em>Connect</em>. Your wallet will switch to Electroneum EVM if needed.</li>
    <li><strong>Fund gas</strong> — make sure you hold a little ETN for transactions (and any ERC-20 you plan to list in).</li>
    <li><strong>Verify addresses</strong> — cross-check spender/contract targets with <a href="/references/contract-addresses">Contract Addresses</a>.</li>
    <li><strong>Start</strong> — choose a creator flow below, or jump to the collector guides.</li>
  </ol>
</Steps>

## Essentials you should know

- **Fees & rewards:** Marketplace fee is **2.5%** (1.5% → Reward Distributor, 1% → Panthart).  
  See **Creators → <a href="/creators/royalties-fees">Royalties & Fees</a>** and **Governance → <a href="/governance-and-policies/fees-schedule">Fees Schedule</a>**.  
- **Royalties:** Honored during settlement when the collection defines a royalty policy.  
- **Media & metadata:** Best-practice JSON and IPFS guidance: **Creators → <a href="/creators/media-metadata">Media & Metadata</a>**.  
- **Collections:** Edit description, logo, banner, and withdraw proceeds as the owner: **Creators → <a href="/creators/collections-management">Collections Management</a>**.  
- **Safety:** Responsible disclosure and phishing guidance: **Governance → <a href="/governance-and-policies/security-and-disclosure">Security & Disclosure</a>**.
